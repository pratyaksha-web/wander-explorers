Travel Tips

Quick tips to make your trip easier and safer.

  • Carry a small first-aid kit and any regular medicines.
  • Make digital and physical copies of important documents.
  • Learn a few basic phrases of the local language — people appreciate it.
  • Keep local emergency numbers and your hotel contact handy.
